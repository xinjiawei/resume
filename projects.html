AI & Nursing

This project aims at the research and implementation of rehabilitation system for aphasia patients, obtaining user data, generating user ability level and establishing user database. Based on the ability level, training task and difficulty level are set, multi-channel stimulus signal of training topic is sent and feedback signal is received, and difficulty level is adjusted adaptively to complete the training task. According to the completion information of the training task, the assessment task is issued, the rehabilitation status is evaluated, and the rehabilitation status is entered into the database. The rehabilitation curve was obtained according to the rehabilitation situation, and the next training task was set based on the training task and rehabilitation curve.
